Package Overview

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the heart of Italy. This curated 7-day escape blends iconic landmarks with authentic cultural experiences. Wander through Roman ruins, marvel at Renaissance masterpieces in Florence, and glide along the enchanting canals of Venice. Perfect for first-time visitors and seasoned travelers alike, this package offers comfortable accommodations, guided tours, and ample free time to explore at your own pace.

Itinerary Highlights

Day 1: Arrival in Rome & Ancient Wonders

Arrive in Rome, transfer to your hotel. Afternoon guided tour of the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill. Evening at leisure.

Day 2: Vatican City & Roman Charm

Morning visit to Vatican City, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Vatican Museums (Sistine Chapel). Afternoon free to explore Trastevere or toss a coin in the Trevi Fountain.

Day 3: High-Speed Train to Florence & Renaissance Art

Travel by high-speed train to Florence. Check into your hotel. Afternoon walking tour including the Duomo, Ponte Vecchio, and Piazza della Signoria. Skip-the-line entry to the Accademia Gallery (David).

Day 4: Florence & Tuscan Flavors (Optional Tour)

Day at leisure in Florence to explore the Uffizi Gallery, climb Giotto's Bell Tower, or shop for leather goods. Optional afternoon tour to a Tuscan winery for tasting.

Day 5: Journey to Venice & Canal Charm

Travel by train to Venice. Transfer to your hotel via water taxi (what an arrival!). Afternoon guided tour of St. Mark's Square, St. Mark's Basilica, and the Doge's Palace.

Day 6: Venetian Gondola Ride & Islands (Optional)

Morning gondola ride through the charming canals. Afternoon free to explore hidden corners, visit Rialto Market, or take an optional boat trip to Murano and Burano islands.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a final Italian breakfast before transferring to Venice Marco Polo Airport (VCE) for your departure.

What's Included

  • Accommodation for 6 nights in centrally located 3/4-star hotels.
  • Daily breakfast at hotels.
  • High-speed train tickets: Rome to Florence, Florence to Venice.
  • Guided tours as mentioned in the itinerary (Rome, Florence, Venice).
  • Skip-the-line entrance fees for Colosseum/Forum, Vatican Museums, Accademia Gallery.
  • Gondola ride in Venice.
  • Airport/hotel transfers as specified (Arrival Rome, Departure Venice).
  • Local taxes and service charges.

What's Not Included

  • International airfare to Rome / from Venice.
  • Lunches, dinners, and drinks (unless specified).
  • Optional tours and activities (Tuscan winery, Murano/Burano trip).
  • Travel insurance (highly recommended).
  • City taxes (payable locally at hotels, typically €2-€6 per person/night).
  • Personal expenses, tips, and gratuities.
